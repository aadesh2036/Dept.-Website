About the College:
Shivprasad Sadanand Jaiswal College was established in the year 1990 by Shri Durga Shikshan Sanstha, Arjuni Morgaon Dist. Gondia. The institution is permanently affiliated with Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur and got recognized under sections 2(f) and 12(B) of the UGC Act of 1956. Our college is reaccredited with ‘B’ grade by the National Assessment & Accreditation Council (NAAC) in 2019 in the 3rd cycle with a CGPA of 2.11.

Shivprasad Sadanand Jaiswal College intends to contribute the finest careers and bright future for the students. With uninterrupted guidance, the students upgrade their knowledge and make them be fit for obtaining Career Opportunities. The College offers BA, B.Sc., and B.Com. along with various certificate courses, value added and skill baed courses for the students to pursue education in and out of the classroom. Outstanding teaching methodology, knowledgeable and research-oriented faculty, a well-structured and a lush green campus – all contribute to the making of competent and innovative students and transformational citizens for the future. The extensive use of ICT tools for the effective delivery of the syllabi has transformed the students learning capacity and outcomes.

The institution’s motivation is based on the noble concept derived from the Rigveda, " आनो भद्रा ऋतवो यन्तु विश्वतः " It means “Let all knowledge in the world come to us.” By adhering to this vision, the institution is pursuing qualitative outcomes and excellence in higher education in Maharashtra’s eastern region.
